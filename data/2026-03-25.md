### [1] [Far-field compressive ultrasound beamforming](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22496)
*Nikunj Khetan,Jerome Mertz*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 提出KK波束形成方法，通过远场分解将CPWC超声成像转换至k空间，采用游标型采样策略优化对比度与分辨率的权衡，实现10倍数据压缩同时保持图像质量与传统DAS相当，并显著提升计算效率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 针对CPWC超声成像的数据冗余问题，旨在通过压缩感知减少采样量，在维持图像质量的前提下优化对比度与分辨率的平衡，并提高计算速度。

Method: 通过对接收射频数据进行远场分解得到虚拟平面波，将成像过程完全重构于k空间域；基于共阵原理设计游标型采样策略，包括低频密集采样、频带移位扩展和共焦/混合复合方案，实现对k空间采样的直接灵活控制。

Result: 利用校准体模和活体人体组织数据验证，实现约一个数量级的压缩因子（10倍），图像质量与传统延时叠加(DAS)相当；同时内存占用减少、缓存效率提升，计算速度显著提高。

Conclusion: KK波束形成方法通过k空间域优化采样策略，成功实现了超声成像数据的高效压缩与计算加速，在保持图像质量的同时提供了新的技术路径，具有重要应用价值。

Abstract: We present a compressive beamforming method for coherent plane-wave compounding (CPWC) ultrasound imaging based on a far-field decomposition of the received radiofrequency (RF) data into virtual plane waves. This decomposition recasts the imaging operation entirely in the spatial frequency domain ($k$-space), allowing direct and flexible control over $k$-space sampling distributions based on the principle of coarrays. We present vernier-type sampling strategies designed to optimize the tradeoff between image contrast and resolution with minimum redundancy, including strategies that favor dense low-frequency sampling for high contrast, shifted schemes that extend the frequency support for improved resolution, and confocal or hybrid compounding schemes that approximate the spatial-frequency transfer function of conventional DAS beamforming. Our method, called KK beamforming, is validated with a calibration phantom and in-vivo human tissue data, demonstrating compression factors of an order of magnitude while maintaining image qualities comparable to conventional DAS. We further demonstrate that KK beamforming yields improvements in computational speed owing to its reduced memory footprint and more efficient cache utilization of the compressed data and associated look-up tables.

### [2] [Extended-Target Classification and Localization for Near-Field ISAC](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.23093)
*Zongyao Zhao,Zhaolin Wang,Lincong Han,Jing Jin,Yuanwei Liu,Kaibin Huang*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 针对近场ISAC中扩展目标联合分类与定位问题，本文提出双分支推理框架，通过跨任务注意力实现特征互补，结合不确定性感知回归与物理引导约束提升定位性能，在共享OFDM基准下以更少的感知音调达到最优联合工作点。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有近场ISAC方法多依赖点目标模型，对实际扩展目标的研究不足，难以满足真实场景需求。

Method: 提出双分支推理框架：语义分支处理分类，几何分支处理定位；在任务特定编码后引入跨任务注意力交换互补信息；采用不确定性感知回归和包含平面一致性、峰值响应正则化及几何耦合约束的物理引导目标函数提升定位精度。

Result: 实验表明，跨任务注意力主要提升分类性能，而强定位性能需依赖不确定性感知与结构化监督；在相同性能区域，所提框架使用更少感知音调即达最佳联合工作点。

Conclusion: 该框架有效解决了扩展目标的联合感知问题，通过特征互补与物理约束实现性能提升，为近场ISAC系统提供了实用化方案。

Abstract: Near-field integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) enables object-level sensing from distance-dependent array responses, yet most existing near-field methods still rely on point-target models and realistic extended targets remain largely unexplored. In this paper, joint target classification and range-azimuth localization are studied from channel responses of realistic extended targets. A dual-branch inference framework is proposed. Semantic and geometric branches are used for classification and localization, respectively. Cross-task attention is introduced after task-specific encoding so that complementary cues can be exchanged without forcing full feature sharing from the input stage. To improve localization on the same backbone, uncertainty-aware regression and a physics-guided structured objective are adopted, including planar consistency, peak-response regularization, and geometry-coupling constraints. Training and evaluation data are generated from full-wave electromagnetic scattering simulations of voxelized vehicle targets with randomized heading angles, material contrasts, and placements. The compared variants show that cross-task attention mainly benefits classification, while uncertainty-aware and structured supervision are needed to recover strong localization performance on the same backbone. Under the adopted shared-OFDM benchmark, the proposed framework reaches the best joint operating point with fewer sensing tones for the same target performance region.

### [3] [Geometric Direction Finding on Dynamic Manifolds: Unambiguous DOA Estimation for Spatially Undersampled UWB Arrays](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.23267)
*Kailun Tian,Kaili Jiang,Dechang Wang,Hancong Feng,Yuxin Zhao,Ying Xiong,Bin Tang*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 该论文提出一种动态流形视角，用于解决UWB电磁感知中空间欠采样、异步阵列相位和波束斜率三大物理约束的联合问题。通过将UWB信号观测建模为高维空间中的连续流形曲线，理论证明DOA仅由流形几何形状唯一确定，构建包含外在向量参数与内在标量不变量的几何参数体系，实现无校准相位误差下的高精度DOA估计，并消除稀疏阵列的空间模糊。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统DOA估计方法难以同时处理UWB传感中的空间欠采样、异步阵列相位和波束斜率三大约束，现有方案多孤立解决，导致复杂场景性能受限。为此，亟需一种统一的理论框架来协同克服这些物理限制。

Method: 将UWB信号观测视为由时间演化和阵列拓扑驱动的高维连续流形曲线，从理论上证明DOA仅取决于流形的几何形状而非绝对到达相位。基于此，构建包含外在向量参数和内在标量不变量的几何参数体系，并设计相应的DOA估计算法。外在参数作为传统阵列处理的动态扩展，提升自由度以抑制栅瓣；内在不变量提供与相位模型无关的鲁棒性。

Result: 仿真结果表明，所推导的几何参数解析表达式与数值真值高度吻合。该框架在稀疏阵列下完全消除了空间模糊，并在无校准相位误差条件下实现了高精度测向。

Conclusion: 动态流形视角为UWB电磁感知中的三大物理约束提供了统一的解决方案，显著提升了DOA估计的鲁棒性和精度，为未来宽带阵列信号处理开辟了新的研究路径。

Abstract: Traditional Direction of Arrival (DOA) estimation methods struggle to simultaneously address three physical constraints in Ultra-Wideband (UWB) electromagnetic sensing: spatial undersampling, asynchronous array phase, and beam squint. Existing solutions treat these issues in isolation, leading to limited performance in complex scenarios. This paper proposes a novel dynamic manifold perspective, which models UWB signal observations as a continuous manifold curve in a high-dimensional space driven by temporal evolution and array topology. We theoretically demonstrate that the DOA can be uniquely determined solely by the geometric shape of the manifold, rather than the absolute arrival phase. Based on this perspective, we construct a geometric parameter system comprising extrinsic and intrinsic parameters, along with a corresponding DOA estimation framework. Extrinsic vector parameters serve as a dynamic extension of traditional array processing, effectively expanding the degrees of freedom to suppress grating lobes. Intrinsic scalar invariants provide a new geometric perspective independent of traditional phase models, offering intrinsic robustness against array channel phase errors. Simulation results show that the derived analytical expressions for geometric parameters are highly consistent with numerical truths. The proposed framework not only completely eliminates spatial ambiguity in sparse arrays but also achieves high-precision direction finding under conditions with calibration-free phase errors.
