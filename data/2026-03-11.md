### [1] [Two-Stage Hybrid Transceiver Design Relying on Low-Resolution ADCs in Partially Connected MU Terahertz (THz) MIMO Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09282)
*Abhisha Garg,Akash Kumar,Suraj Srivastava,Aditya K. Jagannatham,Lajos Hanzo*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 针对低分辨率多用户太赫兹大规模MIMO系统，提出一种基于部分连接架构的两级混合收发器设计，通过建立包含吸收、反射和自由空间损耗的精确信道模型，并利用少量真时延线的新型波束成形技术消除波束分裂效应，在频谱效率上实现约13%的性能提升。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 太赫兹大规模MIMO系统由于高带宽和大规模天线阵列，面临空间宽带效应和频率宽带效应联合导致的"双宽带效应"问题，传统方法难以有效解决，且现有架构在频谱效率上仍有提升空间。

Method: 设计两级混合收发器结构并采用部分连接架构；针对每个用户子阵列建立包含吸收、反射和自由空间损耗的太赫兹MIMO信道精确模型；提出基于少量真时延(TTD)线的新型波束成形技术，消除频域上的波束分裂效应。

Result: 仿真结果表明，所提方案相比现有先进技术在频谱效率方面获得了约13%的性能提升。

Conclusion: 该研究通过精确建模和创新的波束成形设计，有效解决了太赫兹系统的双宽带效应问题，显著提升了系统频谱效率，为低分辨率太赫兹大规模MIMO系统的实际应用提供了可行方案。

Abstract: A two-stage hybrid transceiver is designed by considering a partially connected architecture at the base station (BS) for a low-resolution multi-user (MU) THz massive multiple input multiple output (MIMO) system. Due to its high bandwidth coupled with a high number of antennas, the THz band suffers from the deleterious spatial-wideband and frequency-wideband effects jointly termed as the dual-wideband effect. To address this undesired phenomenon, we rigorously model the THz MIMO channel at each subarray corresponding to each user by incorporating the absorption, reflection, and free-space losses. Subsequently, a novel beamforming technique is proposed that employs only a few true time delay (TTD) lines for eliminating the beam-split effect, which is the manifestation of the spatial-wideband effect in the frequency domain. Our simulation results demonstrate a performance improvement of around 13% in terms of spectral efficiency over the existing state-of-the-art techniques.

### [2] [Low-Rank Cyclostationarity Predictive Routing Is Almost as Good as Real-Time Data-based Routing](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09579)
*Oriel-Singer,Ilai-Bistritz,Giseung-Park,Woohyeon-Byeon,Youngchul-Sung,Amir-Leshem*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 提出一种基于交通矩阵低秩分解和时域子空间系数的时空预测器，用于离线交通规划。在首尔路网的实测表明，平均额外通行时间小于1.5分钟，且尾部分布与近实时预测器相当。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 动态最短路径路由依赖实时数据，但离线交通规划任务（如自适应拥堵定价、车队路由和长期运营决策）需要基于预测的交通估计器。现有实时路由方法不适用于离线决策场景。

Method: 开发基于交通矩阵低秩分解的时空预测器，利用低秩特性捕捉交通流的时空相关性，结合时域子空间系数进行预测建模。

Result: 在首尔路网的测量实验中，预测器平均额外通行时间小于1.5分钟；额外通行时间分布的尾部与近实时预测器匹配；一年期交通数据仿真验证了方法的鲁棒性。

Conclusion: 该预测器在精度上接近实时预测器，同时满足离线规划需求，为交通管理决策提供了实用工具。

Abstract: Dynamic shortest-path routing, using real-time traffic data, enables path selection responsive to evolving conditions. Nevertheless, transportation planning tasks such as adaptive congestion pricing, fleet routing, and long-term operational decisions rely on offline traffic estimators. To address this problem, we develop a spatiotemporal predictor based on a low-rank decomposition of the traffic matrix and the temporal subspace coefficients. Using a recent large-scale measurement campaign over the Seoul road network, we show that our proposed predictor incurs an average excess travel time of less than 1.5 minutes. Moreover, our predictor's tail of the excess travel time distribution matches that of a near-real-time predictor. Results based on one year of traffic data are also demonstrated in simulations.

Abstract: Finetuning wireless receivers to a specific deployment scenario can yield significant error-rate performance improvements without increasing processing complexity. However, site-specific finetuning has so far only been demonstrated on synthetic channel data and lacks real-world benchmarks. In this work, we empirically study site-specific finetuning of neural receivers using real-world 5G NR physical uplink shared channel (PUSCH) data collected with an over-the-air testbed at ETH Zurich across three scenarios: (i) a small laboratory, (ii) a large office floor, and (iii) a high-mobility outdoor environment. Our results confirm substantial error-rate performance improvements from site-specific finetuning, consistent with earlier findings based on synthetic channel data. Moreover, we demonstrate that these improvements generalize across different user-equipment hardware and deployment scenarios.

*Junchi Liu,Zijun Wang,Rui Zhang*

TL;DR: 提出基于线性bandit和Thompson采样的近场通信波束训练框架，通过DFT域相关高斯先验和三种搜索策略，实现90%导频开销降低与2dB以上SNR增益，连续空间搜索具有渐近最优性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 多径信道下近场通信的高效波束训练面临导频开销限制与性能优化的双重挑战，需在探索与利用间取得平衡并挖掘空间结构信息。

Method: 采用线性bandit建模，利用Thompson Sampling平衡探索与利用；在DFT域设计高斯核相关先验捕捉空间相关性与近场能量泄漏；开发码本约束搜索、连续空间搜索及两阶段混合精化三种策略。

Abstract: This letter proposes a linear bandit-based beam training framework for near-field communication under multi-path channels. By leveraging Thompson Sampling (TS), the framework adaptively balances exploration and exploitation to maximize cumulative beamforming gain under limited pilot overhead. To ensure data-efficient learning, we incorporate a correlated Gaussian prior in the DFT domain, using a Gaussian kernel to capture spatial correlations and near-field energy leakage. We develop three TS strategies: codebook-constrained search for rapid convergence via structural regularization, continuous-space search to achieve near-optimal performance, and a two-stage hybrid refinement scheme that balances convergence speed and estimation accuracy. Simulation results show that the proposed framework reduces pilot overhead by up to 90\% while achieving more than a 2dB SNR gain over baselines in multipath environments. Furthermore, the continuous-space search is shown to be asymptotically optimal, approaching the full-CSI bound when the pilot overhead is unconstrained.
