### [1] [Probabilistic Occupancy Grid for Radio-Based SLAM](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03559)
*Xuhong Li,Erik Leitinger,Fredrik Tufvesson,Florian Meyer*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 针对6G及未来系统中射频感知的简化几何模型局限性，本文提出一种概率占据栅格框架，通过多径SLAM联合重建环境的几何结构与射频特性，实现物理可解释的无线电地图构建。

Motivation: 现有RF传感方法依赖点散射体或平面假设以保持推理可行性，但这些简化表示缺乏物理信息性，无法准确建模复杂形状的扩展物体。6G系统需要兼具通信与环境感知能力，亟需更精确的物理感知模型。

Method: 将占据栅格地图表示集成到多径SLAM框架中：采用表面模型连接射频测量与网格地图，用占据状态捕捉测量不确定性和细粒度几何细节，并通过反射系数建模物体的射频属性，实现移动体定位与环境地图的联合估计。

Conclusion: 该工作为物理可解释的射频地图构建提供了原理性概念验证方法，推动了6G及未来无线系统中通信-感知一体化的发展，在移动Agent定位与环境建图方面展现出良好应用前景。

Abstract: Sensing is an integral part of 6G and beyond systems, providing exceptional environmental perception along with communication. RF-based sensing often relies on simplified geometric assumptions (e.g., point scatterers or planar surfaces) to model specular multipath and keep inference tractable. However, such representations are not physically informative and fail to accurately capture extended objects with complex shapes and properties. This paper presents a probabilistic occupancy grid framework for radio-based simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM), jointly reconstructing geometric structures and their radio-related properties. The proposed occupancy grid map representation is integrated into a multipath-based SLAM (MP-SLAM) formulation to enable simultaneous mobile-agent localization and environment mapping using multipath measurements.
  To connect RF measurements with the grid map, a surface model is employed to describe candidate reflection paths, while occupancy grid cell states capture measurement uncertainties and fine--grained geometric details. Object RF-related properties are modeled via reflection coefficients. The proposed framework offers a principled, proof-of-concept approach to physically interpretable radio-based mapping, and simulation results demonstrate accurate reconstruction of geometry and material properties, as well as high-accuracy localization. In addition, the results highlight the potential to use prior occupancy maps obtained from other radio devices or complementary sensors for subsequent map extension and refinement.

### [2] [Scalable and Convergent Generalized Power Iteration Precoding for Massive MIMO Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03708)
*Seunghyeong Yoo,Mintaek Oh,Jeonghun Park,Namyoon Lee,Jinseok Choi*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种广义幂迭代预编码(GPIP)框架，用于解决大规模MIMO系统中频谱效率与计算复杂度之间的矛盾。该方法通过利用最优预编码器的低维子空间特性，将高维波束成形问题转化为与用户数而非天线数相关的低维权重优化，在完美和不完美信道状态信息下均显著降低计算复杂度，同时实现最优频谱效率性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 大规模MIMO系统为实现高频谱效率需要先进的预编码算法，但现有算法的计算复杂度随天线数量急剧增长，限制了实际部署。需要开发可扩展且计算高效的预编码方案。

Method: 1. 提出广义幂迭代预编码(GPIP)框架，利用最优预编码器的低维子空间特性，将高维波束成形问题重构为与用户数相关的低维权重优化；2. 针对不完美CSIT场景，证明驻点解存在于估计信道和误差协方差矩阵的联合子空间中，通过低秩近似实现鲁棒设计；3. 应用Sherman-Morrison公式简化矩阵求逆运算；4. 将GPIP更新解释为投影预条件梯度上升法，采用回溯线搜索确保算法稳定性和单调收敛性。

Result: 数值结果表明，与现有先进线性预编码相比，所提方法在显著降低计算复杂度和收敛时间的同时，实现了最高的频谱效率性能，验证了其对大规模MIMO系统的适用性。

Conclusion: 该GPIP框架通过低维子空间优化和高效矩阵运算，成功解决了大规模MIMO预编码的高复杂度问题，在完美和不完美CSIT场景下均能实现高性能、快速收敛的预编码设计，为大规模MIMO系统的实际应用提供了有效解决方案。

Abstract: In massive multiple-input multiple-output (MIMO) systems, achieving high spectral efficiency (SE) often requires advanced precoding algorithms whose complexity scales rapidly with the number of antennas, limiting practical deployment. In this paper, we develop a scalable and computationally efficient generalized power iteration precoding (GPIP) framework for massive MIMO systems under both perfect and imperfect channel state information at the transmitter (CSIT). By exploiting the low-dimensional subspace property of optimal precoders, we reformulate the high-dimensional beamforming problem into a lower-dimensional weight optimization that scales with the number of users rather than antennas. We further extend this framework to the imperfect CSIT scenario by showing that stationary solutions reside in a combined subspace spanned by the estimated channel and error covariance matrices, enabling a robust design via low-rank approximation. To reduce computational cost, we leverage the Sherman-Morrison formula to simplify matrix inversions. Moreover, interpreting the GPIP update as a projected preconditioned gradient ascent method, we establish convergence guarantees and develop a stable and monotonic algorithm using a backtracking line search. Numerical results demonstrate that the proposed methods achieve the highest SE performance compared to state-of-the-art linear precoders with significantly reduced complexity and convergence, highlighting their suitability for large-scale MIMO systems.

### [3] [On the Spectrum of OTFS/VOFDM Signals: PSD Analysis and Bandwidth Allocation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03719)
*Wei Wang,Xiang-Gen Xia*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文揭示了OTFS信号的周期性功率谱密度特性，建立了与OFDM信号的数学关联，并提出了基于零空间的线性预编码方法以实现灵活的带宽分配。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: OTFS/VOFDM被视为下一代移动通信的关键波形，但其频谱特性尚未完全明确，带宽分配方案也未得到充分研究，这对实际系统集成至关重要。

Method: 通过功率谱密度分析揭示离散时间OTFS信号的周期性特征，建立OFDM与OTFS信号功率谱的数学关系，并基于符号-频谱关系设计零空间线性预编码算法。

Result: 发现OTFS信号功率谱以1/(MTs)为周期，在[-1/(2Ts), 1/(2Ts))范围内形成M个相同频谱分量；当信息符号独立时，OTFS功率谱等于CEP-OFDM信号功率谱之和；提出的NSLP方法能有效调控频谱。

Conclusion: 研究阐明了OTFS的固有频谱特性及其带宽分配挑战，建立的数学框架为频谱设计提供理论依据，NSLP方法为实现灵活的OTFS频谱整形提供了可行解决方案。

Abstract: Orthogonal time frequency space (OTFS)/vector OFDM (VOFDM) is widely regarded as a promising waveform for next-generation mobile communications. However, its spectral characteristics are not yet fully understood. The bandwidth allocation scheme, which is crucial for OTFS's integration into practical wireless standards, also remains unexplored. In this paper, we investigate the spectral characteristics of OTFS signals by analyzing their power spectral density (PSD). We demonstrate that the PSD of discrete-time OTFS signals is periodic with a period of $\frac{1}{MT_s}$, where $M$ is the size of the time/Doppler domain in OTFS, a.k.a., the vector size in VOFDM, and $T_s$ is the sampling interval length of digital to analog converter (DAC), resulting in $M$ identical spectral components within the spectral range $[-\frac{1}{2T_s}, \frac{1}{2T_s})$ of the continuous-time OTFS signal. The periodicity makes bandwidth allocation for OTFS/VOFDM signaling substantially challenging. Furthermore, we establish a relationship between the PSD of OFDM signals and that of OTFS signals, revealing that, when the information symbols are independent, the PSD of OTFS signals is equal to the sum of the PSDs of the component-expanded OFDM (CEP-OFDM) signals. Lastly, we derive a relationship between the information symbols and the corresponding OTFS spectrum, and based on which, we propose a null-space-based linear precoding (NSLP) method for OTFS signals to enable flexible bandwidth allocation. Numerical results validate our analytical results regarding the PSD of OTFS signals and show the effectiveness of our proposed NSLP method in tailoring the spectrum of OTFS signals.

Main category: eess.SP
