Motivation: 为满足现代卫星通信对高速数据传输日益增长的需求，Ka波段因其高数据率特性成为研究重点，但面临信号衰减、方向精度、圆极化和阻抗匹配等技术挑战，亟需设计高性能移动天线解决方案。

Method: 采用从单贴片天线单元到8×8平面阵列的递进式设计方法，通过数学建模计算贴片尺寸、基板参数和有效介电常数，利用CST电磁仿真软件进行优化设计，并集成机器学习算法辅助参数优化。

Result: 所设计的Ka波段天线在29 GHz工作频率下，单单元回波损耗低至-30 dB，8×8阵列增益达到21 dB，有效改善了阻抗匹配和辐射性能，验证了相控阵和电子波束扫描配置在解决高频段设计挑战中的有效性。

Conclusion: 本研究成功开发了高性能Ka波段移动天线，推动了高频传输技术进步，为卫星通信系统提供了满足高速互联网接入和多媒体流媒体等应用需求的关键解决方案，具有实际应用价值。

Abstract: This research focuses on the design of Ka-band mobile antennas for satellite communication operating at 29 GHz. Starting from a single element and progressing to an 8x8 array, the antennas achieved a gain of up to 21 dB and return losses as low as -30 dB. The design process involves mathematical calculations and software implementation, utilizing parameters like patch dimensions, substrate properties, and effective permittivity. The chosen Ka-band frequency range, known for higher data transfer rates, addresses the demand for swift communication. Challenges in Ka-band mobile antenna design, including signal attenuation, directional accuracy, circular polarization, and impedance matching, are addressed through various configurations, including phased-array and electronically steerable antennas.
