### [1] [BrainRVQ: A High-Fidelity EEG Foundation Model via Dual-Domain Residual Quantization and Hierarchical Autoregression](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.16951)
*Mingzhe Cui,Tao Chen,Yang Jiao,Yiqin Wang,Lei Xie,Yi Pan,Luca Mainardi*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文提出BrainRVQ，一种基于大规模临床脑电图数据预训练的通用EEG基础模型。该模型采用双域残差向量量化(DD-RVQ)分词器将时域波形和频谱模式解耦为分层离散码，通过层次化自回归预训练目标进行粗到细重构，并利用重要性引导的课程掩码策略优先处理信息丰富的神经事件而非背景噪声。在8个不同下游数据集上的实验表明，BrainRVQ优于现有最先进方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 脑电信号具有低信噪比和复杂的时频谱非平稳性，现有方法往往忽视神经动力学中的分层潜在结构，导致对细粒度信息重构不佳，限制了通用EEG基础模型的发展。

Method: 提出BrainRVQ模型，核心包括：1)双域残差向量量化(DD-RVQ)分词器，将时域波形和频谱模式解耦为分层离散码；2)层次化自回归预训练目标，以粗到细方式学习重构这些码；3)重要性引导的课程掩码策略，优先处理信息丰富的神经事件而非背景噪声。

Result: 在8个不同的下游数据集上进行了广泛实验，结果表明BrainRVQ持续优于现有最先进基线方法，验证了其学习鲁棒且可泛化神经表征的有效性。代码和模型权重已开源。

Conclusion: BrainRVQ成功开发了适用于EEG的通用基础模型，通过分层离散化和课程学习策略有效解决了EEG信号的低信噪比和非平稳性挑战，为神经动力学表征学习提供了新思路，并在多个下游任务上展现了优越性能。

Abstract: Developing foundation models for electroencephalography (EEG) remains challenging due to the signal's low signal-to-noise ratio and complex spectro-temporal non-stationarity. Existing approaches often overlook the hierarchical latent structure inherent in neural dynamics, leading to suboptimal reconstruction of fine-grained information. In this work, we propose BrainRVQ, a general-purpose EEG foundation model pre-trained on a large-scale corpus of clinical EEG data. Unlike standard masked modeling, BrainRVQ features a Dual-Domain Residual Vector Quantization (DD-RVQ) tokenizer that disentangles temporal waveforms and spectral patterns into hierarchical discrete codes. We further introduce a hierarchical autoregressive pre-training objective that learns to reconstruct these codes in a coarse-to-fine manner, utilizing an importance-guided curriculum masking strategy to prioritize information-rich neural events over background noise. Extensive experiments across 8 diverse downstream datasets demonstrate that BrainRVQ consistently outperforms state-of-the-art baselines, validating its effectiveness in learning robust and generalizable neural representations. Our code and model weights are available:https://github.com/keqicmz/BrainRVQ

### [2] [A Novel Near-Field Dictionary Design for Hybrid MIMO with Uniform Planar Arrays](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.17202)
*Luca Antonelli,Antonio Alberto D Amico,Luca Sanguinetti*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 针对近场超大规模MIMO系统中的信道估计挑战，本文提出了一种适用于地面用户场景的新型近场网格设计方案，通过将网格点设置在参考平面上并结合信道精度度量，显著提升了信道估计准确性和频谱效率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 近场超大规模MIMO系统虽能提供增强的空间分辨率，但在混合架构下信道估计面临困难。现有基于字典的信道估计方案通常采用全三维覆盖字典，而当用户设备主要位于地面时，这种设计造成不必要的资源浪费和效率低下，亟需针对地面用户场景的专用优化方案。

Method: 提出一种创新的近场网格设计，将网格点分布在相对于U-MIMO系统处于任意高度的参考平面上，系统采用均匀平面阵列。引入信道精度度量指标来优化码本性能，并阐明传统远场角度采样方法在近场区域的局限性。

Result: 仿真结果表明，在用户设备不远离参考平面的条件下，所提网格设计在信道估计精度和频谱效率方面均优于现有先进方案。

Conclusion: 本文提出的基于参考平面的近场网格设计有效解决了地面用户场景下的信道估计问题，为近场超大规模MIMO系统的实际应用提供了更高效的解决方案。

Abstract: Near-field ultra-massive MIMO (U-MIMO) systems provide enhanced spatial resolution but present challenges for channel estimation, particularly when hybrid architectures are employed. Within this framework, dictionary-based channel estimation schemes are needed to achieve accurate reconstruction from a reduced set of measurements. However, existing near-field dictionaries generally provide full three-dimensional coverage, which is unnecessary when user equipments are primarily located on the ground. In this paper, we propose a novel near-field grid design tailored to this common scenario. Specifically, grid points lie on a reference plane located at an arbitrary height with respect to the U-MIMO system, equipped with a uniform planar array. Furthermore, a channel accuracy metric is used to improve codebook performance, and to remark the limitations of the traditional far-field angular sampling in the near field. Results show that, as long as user equipments are not far from the reference plane, the proposed grid outperforms state-of-the-art designs in both channel estimation accuracy and spectral efficiency.

### [3] [Failure Detection for Pinching-Antenna Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.17257)
*Chongjun Ouyang,Hao Jiang,Zhaolin Wang,Yuanwei Liu,Zhiguo Ding*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 提出基于信号处理的框架检测分段波导 pinched 天线系统的随机分段故障，通过标记导频解耦信号并提供分段可观测性，开发低复杂度最大似然检测器和压缩感知检测器，分别在导频长度足够和不足时接近联合ML性能并优于基线方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 分段波导 pinched 天线系统存在随机分段故障检测难题，需解决被动合并上行信号的解耦问题和实现各分段独立可观测性，传统方法难以在导频资源有限条件下实现高效检测。

Method: 1. 标记导频技术：为每个分段附加标签，施加已知低速率调制，赋予各分段独特签名；2. 当导频长度≥分段数时，设计低复杂度分段最大似然(ML)检测器；3. 当导频长度<分段数时，利用故障指示向量的稀疏性，采用压缩感知检测器。

Result: 数值结果表明：分段检测器性能接近联合ML检测器；压缩感知检测器在短导频下实现可靠检测，性能优于需要更长导频的基线方法。

Conclusion: 该框架通过标记导频有效解决了分段可观测性问题，两种检测方案在不同导频长度条件下均表现优异，为分段天线系统故障检测提供了实用且高效的解决方案。

Abstract: A signal processing-based framework is proposed for detecting random segment failures in segmented waveguide-enabled pinching-antenna systems. To decouple the passively combined uplink signal and to provide per-segment observability, tagged pilots are employed. A simple tag is attached to each segment and is used to apply a known low-rate modulation at the segment feed, which assigns a unique signature to each segment. Based on the tagged-pilot model, a low-complexity per-segment maximum-likelihood (ML) detector is developed for the case in which the pilot length is no smaller than the number of segments. For the case in which the pilot length is smaller than the number of segments, sparsity in the failure-indicator vector is exploited and a compressive sensing-based detector is adopted. Numerical results show that the per-segment detector approaches joint ML performance, while the compressive sensing-based detector achieves reliable detection with a short pilot and can outperform baselines that require much longer pilots.

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 虽然量化噪声已有解析描述，但量化误差的频谱特性大多依赖统计描述。现有方法缺乏对量化噪声谱结构的直观理解，需要更清晰的数学模型来描述均匀量化过程中的频谱特性。

Abstract: Uniform quantization is a topic that has been extensively studied. We first establish the equivalence of a uniform quantizer with a system based on the bipolar pulse frequency modulation and we define afterwards the Fourier transform of the quantized signal using pulse frequency modulation properties. This model brings a more intuitive understanding of the spectral structure of quantization noise and complements prior research in the topic. The results of the paper can be directly applied to level crossing ADCs with zero-order-hold interpolators, giving an accurate estimation of their performance.

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在实际SAR成像场景中，3D傅里叶域的2D切片采样往往不完整，导致重建图像出现显著伪影。传统方法依赖图像域稀疏性等简单先验进行正则化，效果有限。为此，需要建立更准确的表面散射模型以改善3D SAR成像质量。

Method: 采用神经结构从稀疏散射数据中学习符号距离函数，隐式地编码目标表面；针对从稀疏噪声点云估计光滑表面的不适定性问题，通过在训练阶段对隐式表面进行点采样来实现正则化。

Abstract: Synthetic aperture radar (SAR) is a tomographic sensor that measures 2D slices of the 3D spatial Fourier transform of the scene. In many operational scenarios, the measured set of 2D slices does not fill the 3D space in the Fourier domain, resulting in significant artifacts in the reconstructed imagery. Traditionally, simple priors, such as sparsity in the image domain, are used to regularize the inverse problem. In this paper, we review our recent work that achieves state-of-the-art results in 3D SAR imaging employing neural structures to model the surface scattering that dominates SAR returns. These neural structures encode the surface of the objects in the form of a signed distance function learned from the sparse scattering data. Since estimating a smooth surface from a sparse and noisy point cloud is an ill-posed problem, we regularize the surface estimation by sampling points from the implicit surface representation during the training step. We demonstrate the model's ability to represent target scattering using measured and simulated data from single vehicles and a larger scene with a large number of vehicles. We conclude with future research directions calling for methods to learn complex-valued neural representations to enable synthesizing new collections from the volumetric neural implicit representation.

### [6] [Device-Centric ISAC for Exposure Control via Opportunistic Virtual Aperture Sensing](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.17609)

Motivation: 现有二元接近传感器导致保守功率回退，降低链路质量。MPE法规要求设备靠近人体时降低发射功率，精确的距离测量可实现按比例功率调整，在合规同时提升吞吐量。

Abstract: Regulatory limits on Maximum Permissible Exposure (MPE) require handheld devices to reduce transmit power when operated near the user's body. Current proximity sensors provide only binary detection, triggering conservative power back-off that degrades link quality. This paper develops a device-centric integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) method for the device to measure this distance. The Bayesian Cramér-Rao bound for localization is derived under correlated inertial errors. Numerical results at 28GHz demonstrate centimeter-level accuracy with realistic sensor parameters.
