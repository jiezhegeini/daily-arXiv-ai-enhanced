### [1] [On Performance of Fluid Antenna Relay (FAR)-Assisted AAV-NOMA Wireless Network](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24954)
*Ruopeng Xu,Songling Zhang,Zhaohui Yang,Yixuan Chen,Mingzhe Chen,Zhaoyang Zhang,Kai-Kit Wong*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文研究基于流体天线中继的NOMA下行通信系统性能。通过无人机搭载流体天线中继，采用AF或DF中继方案，利用Copula理论建模信道分布并推导弱用户的中断概率闭式表达式。仿真验证所提方案显著优于无中继基准方案，并分析了不同中继位置和通信中断阈值下的最佳中继方案选择参数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统中继系统在信道条件较差时难以保证弱用户的通信质量。流体天线系统提供额外的自由度，结合NOMA技术可提升频谱效率。无人机搭载的中继具有灵活部署优势，但信道建模复杂。研究流体天线中继辅助的NOMA系统对降低弱用户中断概率、提升系统可靠性具有重要意义。

Method: 构建两阶段传输框架：基站到用户和中继的第一阶段，中继采用AF/DF方案转发信号到弱用户的第二阶段；利用Copula理论特别是高斯Copula函数建模流体天线信道统计分布；推导AF和DF两种中继方案下弱用户中断概率的闭式解析表达式；通过数值仿真验证理论分析并评估不同参数配置下的系统性能。

Result: 成功推导出两种中继方案下弱用户中断概率的解析表达式；仿真结果表明，所提流体天线中继方案一致优于无中继基准方案；揭示了在不同中继位置和通信中断阈值条件下，最佳中继方案选择参数的具体取值规律。

Conclusion: 流体天线中继辅助的NOMA系统能有效降低弱用户中断概率。高斯Copula函数适用于流体天线信道建模。AF和DF方案各有优势，需根据中继位置和QoS要求选择合适方案。该研究为未来灵活高效中继系统的设计提供了理论依据和技术支撑。

Abstract: In this paper, we investigate the performance of a fluid antenna relay (FAR)-assisted downlink communication system utilizing non-orthogonal multiple access (NOMA). The FAR, which integrates a fluid antenna system (FAS), is equipped on an autonomous aerial vehicle (AAV), and introduces extra degrees of freedom to improve the performance of the system. The transmission is divided into a first phase from the base station (BS) to the users and the FAR, and a second phase where the FAR forwards the signal using amplify-and-forward (AF) or decode-and-forward (DF) relaying to reduce the outage probability (OP) for the user maintaining weaker channel conditions. To analyze the OP performance of the weak user, Copula theory and the Gaussian copula function are employed to model the statistical distribution of the FAS channels. Analytical expressions for weak user's OP are derived for both the AF and the DF schemes. Simulation results validate the effectiveness of the proposed scheme, showing that it consistently outperforms benchmark schemes without the FAR. In addition, numerical simulations also demonstrate the values of the relaying scheme selection parameter under different FAR positions and communication outage thresholds.

### [2] [Near-field Beam Training under Multi-path Channels: A Hybrid Learning-and-Optimization Approach](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24960)
*Jiapeng Li,Changsheng You,Guoliang Cheng,Haobin Sun,Chao Zhou,Linglong Dai*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 针对极大规模阵列近场波束训练在复杂多径场景下的性能退化问题，本文提出一种混合深度学习与优化算法的两阶段方法：先用U-Net从接收功率模式获取多径参数的粗估计，再用粒子群优化和Gerchberg-Saxton算法精炼参数，在低计算复杂度下实现高精度估计。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有极大规模阵列近场波束训练方法多基于离散傅里叶变换码本并假设视距信道主导，但在一般多径场景中，复杂的接收信号功率模式会导致通信性能下降，亟需一种高精度、低复杂度的参数估计新方法。

Method: 提出混合学习-优化波束训练框架：第一阶段设计专用U-Net学习接收功率模式到多径角度/距离粗估计的映射，采用置换不变训练策略解决参数匹配歧义，通过路径存在性logits估计路径数；第二阶段在限定区域内使用粒子群优化精炼参数，并用Gerchberg-Saxton算法重构信道增益。

Result: 仿真结果表明，所提方法在参数估计精度和可达速率方面显著优于现有基准方案，同时保持较低的计算复杂度。

Conclusion: 该混合设计有效解决了极大规模阵列多径环境下的近场波束训练问题，实现了估计精度与计算效率的良好平衡，具有实际应用价值。

Abstract: For extremely large-scale arrays (XL-arrays), the discrete Fourier transform (DFT) codebook, conventionally used in the far-field, has recently been employed for near-field beam training. However, most existing methods rely on the line-of-sight (LoS) dominant channel assumption, which may suffer degraded communication performance when applied to the general multi-path scenario due to the more complex received signal power pattern at the user. To address this issue, we propose in this paper a new hybrid learning-and-optimization-based beam training method that first leverages deep learning (DL) to obtain coarse channel parameter estimates, and then refines them via a model-based optimization algorithm, hence achieving high-accuracy estimation with low computational complexity. Specifically, in the first stage, a tailored U-Net architecture is developed to learn the non-linear mapping from the received power pattern to coarse estimates of the angles and ranges of multi-path components. In particular, the inherent permutation ambiguity in multi-path parameter matching is effectively resolved by a permutation invariant training (PIT) strategy, while the unknown number of paths is estimated based on defined path existence logits. In the second stage, we further propose an efficient particle swarm optimization method to refine the angular and range parameters within a confined search region; in the meanwhile, a Gerchberg-Saxton algorithm is used to retrieve multi-path channel gains from the received power pattern. Last, numerical results demonstrate that the proposed hybrid design significantly outperforms various benchmarks in terms of parameter estimation accuracy and achievable rate, yet with low computational complexity.
