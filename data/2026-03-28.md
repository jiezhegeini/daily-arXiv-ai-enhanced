### [1] [FED-HARGPT: A Hybrid Centralized-Federated Approach of a Transformer-based Architecture for Human Context Recognition](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24601)
*Wandemberg Gibaut,Alexandre Osorio,Amparo Munoz,Sildolfo F. G. Neto,Fabio Grassiotto*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本研究提出了一种基于Transformer的混合集中式-联邦学习框架，用于利用边缘设备传感器数据进行人类活动识别，在保护非独立同分布数据隐私的同时，实现了与集中式训练相当的模型性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 随着智能手机和可穿戴设备的普及，大量涉及个人隐私的传感器数据（如惯性测量单元数据）在边缘端持续生成。传统集中式训练方法需要汇集原始数据，存在隐私泄露风险。联邦学习虽能保护隐私，但面临非独立同分布数据挑战，需要在隐私保护与模型性能间取得平衡。

Method: 研究采用Flower框架构建联邦学习系统，以集中式训练模型为基线，设计基于Transformer架构的混合集中式-联邦学习方案。通过在非独立同分布数据场景下评估联邦模型训练过程，对比分析模型准确性和鲁棒性。

Result: 实验结果表明，该混合方法在保持数据隐私的前提下，显著提升了人类活动识别模型的准确性和鲁棒性。联邦学习设置实现了与集中式模型相当的性能表现，证实了其在真实应用场景中的有效性。

Conclusion: 联邦学习在人类活动识别领域展现出巨大潜力，能够在保护用户数据隐私的同时维持模型高性能，为非独立同分布数据下的边缘智能应用提供了可行的技术路径。

Abstract: The study explores a hybrid centralized-federated approach for Human Activity Recognition (HAR) using a Transformer-based architecture. With the increasing ubiquity of edge devices, such as smartphones and wearables, a significant amount of private data from wearable and inertial sensors is generated, facilitating discreet monitoring of human activities, including resting, sleeping, and walking. This research focuses on deploying HAR technologies using mobile sensor data and leveraging Federated Learning within the Flower framework to evaluate the training of a federated model derived from a centralized baseline. The experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed hybrid approach in improving the accuracy and robustness of HAR models while preserving data privacy in a non-IID data scenario. The federated learning setup demonstrated comparable performance to centralized models, highlighting the potential of federated learning to strike a balance between data privacy and model performance in real-world applications.

### [2] [On Performance of Fluid Antenna Relay (FAR)-Assisted AAV-NOMA Wireless Network](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24954)
*Ruopeng Xu,Songling Zhang,Zhaohui Yang,Yixuan Chen,Mingzhe Chen,Zhaoyang Zhang,Kai-Kit Wong*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 该论文研究基于无人机流体天线中继(FAR)的NOMA下行通信系统，通过高斯Copula理论建模流体天线信道，分析放大转发与解码转发中继策略下弱用户的中断概率性能，验证了该方案相对于无中继系统的性能优势。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 利用无人机搭载流体天线中继引入额外自由度，提升NOMA下行系统性能，降低弱用户的中断概率，满足更高可靠性的通信需求。

Method: 建立两阶段传输模型，第一阶段从基站到用户与中继，第二阶段由中继采用放大转发或解码转发策略转发信号；采用Copula理论中的高斯Copula函数对流体天线信道的统计分布进行建模；推导弱用户中断概率的闭式解析表达式。

Result: 获得了解析的弱用户中断概率表达式；仿真结果表明所提方案始终优于无中继的基准方案；数值分析给出了不同中继位置和通信中断阈值下的最佳中继方案选择参数。

Conclusion: 流体天线中继辅助的NOMA系统能有效降低弱用户的中断概率，性能显著优于传统无中继方案，且可根据实际部署场景选择合适的中继转发策略。

Abstract: In this paper, we investigate the performance of a fluid antenna relay (FAR)-assisted downlink communication system utilizing non-orthogonal multiple access (NOMA). The FAR, which integrates a fluid antenna system (FAS), is equipped on an autonomous aerial vehicle (AAV), and introduces extra degrees of freedom to improve the performance of the system. The transmission is divided into a first phase from the base station (BS) to the users and the FAR, and a second phase where the FAR forwards the signal using amplify-and-forward (AF) or decode-and-forward (DF) relaying to reduce the outage probability (OP) for the user maintaining weaker channel conditions. To analyze the OP performance of the weak user, Copula theory and the Gaussian copula function are employed to model the statistical distribution of the FAS channels. Analytical expressions for weak user's OP are derived for both the AF and the DF schemes. Simulation results validate the effectiveness of the proposed scheme, showing that it consistently outperforms benchmark schemes without the FAR. In addition, numerical simulations also demonstrate the values of the relaying scheme selection parameter under different FAR positions and communication outage thresholds.

### [3] [Efficient compressive sensing for machinery vibration signals](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25166)
*Imen Tounsi,Fadi Karkafi,Mohammed El Badaoui,François Guillet*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 该研究对比分析了压缩感知框架中的关键组件，并提出了一种适用于机械振动信号的硬件高效Wang测量矩阵，实验表明其在高压缩比下比传统矩阵具有更高的重构质量。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 机械振动监测需要高采样率，导致数据量大、存储、传输和功耗受限；压缩感知利用信号的稀疏性实现亚奈奎斯特采样和高效重构，具有解决上述问题的潜力。

Method: 对稀疏基、测量矩阵和重构算法进行对比分析；首次将Wang矩阵应用于机械振动信号；使用HUMS2023和CETIM齿轮箱数据集进行评估；与传统高斯、伯努利矩阵进行比较。

Result: 在高压缩比下，Wang矩阵的信噪比（SNR）优于高斯与伯努利矩阵，重构质量更佳。

Abstract: Mechanical vibration monitoring often requires high sampling rates and generates large data volumes, posing challenges for storage, transmission, and power efficiency. Compressive Sensing (CS) offers a promising approach to overcome these constraints by exploiting signal sparsity to enable sub-Nyquist acquisition and efficient reconstruction. This study presents a comprehensive comparative analysis of the key components of the CS framework: sparse basis, measurement matrix, and reconstruction algorithm for machinery vibration signals. In addition, a hardware-efficient measurement matrix, the Wang matrix, originally developed for image compression, is introduced and evaluated for the first time in this context. Experimental assessment using the HUMS2023 and the CETIM gearbox datasets demonstrates that this matrix achieves superior reconstruction quality, with higher SNR, compared to conventional Gaussian and Bernoulli matrices, especially at high compression ratios.

### [4] [Multi-User Covert Communication in Spatially Heterogeneous Wireless Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25549)
*Jinyoung Lee,Hyeonsik Yeom*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 本文研究了空间分布式用户的上行多用户隐蔽通信系统，通过显式建模用户几何位置和信道差异，证明了广义开关功率控制是最优策略，推导出检测错误概率和协作用户数的闭式表达式，并提出低复杂度分段搜索算法，揭示了空间异质性可提升隐蔽性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统研究采用平均参数和同构假设近似信道统计特性，无法准确刻画实际空间异质性环境。本文旨在突破这一局限，通过精确建模用户几何位置和差异化信道特性，为隐蔽通信系统提供更真实的性能分析和优化方案。

Method: 1) 建立空间异构的用户几何位置与信道方差模型；2) 数学证明联合考虑Bob和Willie信道的广义开关功率控制策略的最优性；3) 推导检测错误概率和最小协作用户数的闭式表达式；4) 提出低复杂度分段搜索算法实现精确优化；5) 通过蒙特卡洛仿真验证理论分析。

Result: 1) 证明了广义开关功率控制在异构配置下的最优性；2) 获得检测错误概率和协作用户需求的精确闭式解；3) 发现空间异质性可显著增强隐蔽通信性能；4) 算法大幅降低计算复杂度同时保证最优性；5) 异构优化方案性能显著优于同构假设下的优化。

Conclusion: 研究揭示了空间位置差异对隐蔽通信的重要影响，建立的精确模型和优化方法为实际系统部署提供了理论依据，证明考虑空间异质性可带来显著性能增益，所提出的低复杂度算法具有实用价值。

Abstract: This paper investigates an uplink multi-user covert communication system with spatially distributed users. Unlike prior works that approximate channel statistics using averaged parameters and homogeneous assumptions, this study explicitly models each user's geometric position and corresponding user-to-Willie and user-to-Bob channel variances. This approach enables an accurate characterization of spatially heterogeneous covert environments. We mathematically prove that a generalized on-off power control scheme, which jointly accounts for both Bob's and Willie's channels, constitutes the optimal transmission strategy in heterogeneous user configurations. Leveraging the optimal strategy, we derive closed-form expressions for the minimum detection error probability and the minimum number of cooperative users required to satisfy a covert constraint. With the closed-form expressions, comprehensive theoretical analyses are conducted, which are validated by Monte-Carlo simulations. One important insight obtained from the analysis is that user spatial heterogeneity can enhance covert communication performance. Building on these findings, a piecewise search algorithm is proposed to achieve exact optimality with significantly reduced computational complexity. We demonstrate that optimization considering user's spatial heterogeneity achieves substantially improved covert communication performance than that based on the assumption of spatial homogeneity.

### [5] [Challenge-Response Authentication for LEO Satellite Channels: Exploiting Orbit-Specific Uniqueness](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25576)
*Jinyoung Lee,Stefano Tomasin,Dong-Hyun Jung*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 这篇论文提出了一种基于轨道动力学的主动挑战-响应卫星身份认证框架，通过随机采样轨道观测量来建立物理信任根，将认证问题转化为时空一致性验证，有效抵御轨迹感知欺骗攻击。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 随着低地球轨道卫星星座的快速发展，卫星链路在应急通信、导航、广域数据收集和军事行动等关键服务中的作用日益重要。现有被动认证方法存在固定的测量窗口，容易受到攻击。因此，保持这些链路的安全性，特别是验证卫星发射机身份，已成为重要关切。

Method: 提出一种主动挑战-响应认证框架，验证者在随机选择的、事先未知的时间点检查卫星。该框架利用轨道观测量确定性但不可预测的采样特性，建立基于物理的信任根。方法将卫星认证从静态特征匹配转变为受轨道动力学约束的时空一致性验证问题。

Result: 所提框架通过随机采样和轨道动力学约束，提供了强大的身份认证保护，能够有效抵御轨迹感知欺骗攻击。

Conclusion: 该认证方法利用轨道物理特性，通过时空一致性验证实现了卫星身份的强认证，为LEO卫星系统提供了重要的安全保障。

Abstract: The number of low Earth orbit (LEO) satellite constellations has grown rapidly in recent years, bringing a major change to global wireless communications. As LEO satellite links take on a growing role in critical services such as emergency communications, navigation, wide-area data collection, and military operations, keeping these links secure has become an important concern. In particular, verifying the identity of a satellite transmitter is now a basic requirement for protecting the services that rely on satellite access. In this article, we propose an active challenge-response authentication framework in which the verifier checks the satellite at randomly chosen times that are not known in advance, removing the fixed measurement window that existing passive methods expose to adversaries. The proposed framework uses the deterministic yet unpredictably sampled nature of orbital observables to establish a physics based root of trust for satellite identity authentication. This approach transforms satellite authentication from static feature matching into a spatiotemporal consistency verification problem inherently constrained by orbital dynamics, providing robust protection even against trajectory-aware spoofing attacks.
