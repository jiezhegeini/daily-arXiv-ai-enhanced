### [1] [KD-EKF: Knowledge-Distilled Adaptive Covariance EKF for Robust UWB/PDR Indoor Localization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.18027)
*Kyeonghyun Yoo,Wooyong Jung,Namkyung Yoon,Sangmin Lee,Sanghong Kim,Hwangnam Kim*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 该论文针对超宽带(UWB)与惯性测量单元(IMU)足式航迹推算(PDR)融合室内定位中，因固定测量协方差导致在异构环境下鲁棒性差的问题，提出一种基于知识蒸馏的自适应协方差缩放框架(KD-EKF)。通过离线教师模型从历史轨迹学习时序一致性预测，蒸馏至实时学生模型动态调节扩展卡尔曼滤波(EKF)测量协方差，实验证明该方法显著降低定位误差，抑制通视(LOS)/非视距(NLOS)切换时的误差尖峰，减轻长期漂移。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: UWB室内定位在非视距(NLOS)条件下可靠性严重退化，产生米级测距误差且不确定性特征不一致；IMU-based PDR虽可无基础设施互补，但误差随时间非线性累积。基于扩展卡尔曼滤波(EKF)和粒子滤波(PF)的融合方法虽能提升平均精度，但依赖手动调优的固定测量协方差，难以适应变化的室内布局、NLOS比例和运动模式，导致在异构环境中建模测量不确定性的鲁棒性和泛化能力有限。

Method: 提出自适应测量协方差缩放框架：离线阶段利用历史UWB/PDR轨迹训练大型教师模型，生成时序一致的下一位置预测；通过知识蒸馏将教师模型行为迁移至轻量级学生模型，实现实时部署；学生模型基于预测残差连续调节EKF测量协方差，使融合过程具备环境感知能力，无需人工重调参。

Result: 实验结果表明，与固定参数EKF相比，KD-EKF框架显著降低了定位误差，有效抑制了LOS/NLOS过渡期间的误差尖峰，减轻了长期漂移现象，并在多样化室内环境中提升了测量鲁棒性。

Conclusion: 该研究通过知识蒸馏机制实现了测量协方差的自适应动态调节，解决了传统融合方法在复杂室内环境中泛化能力不足的核心问题，为构建高鲁棒性室内定位系统提供了有效解决方案，具有重要的理论价值和实用前景。

Abstract: Ultra-wideband (UWB) indoor localization provides centimeter-level accuracy and low latency, but its measurement reliability degrades severely under Non-Line-of-Sight (NLOS) conditions, leading to meter-scale ranging errors and inconsistent uncertainty characteristics. Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU)-based Pedestrian Dead Reckoning (PDR) complements UWB by providing infrastructure-free motion estimation; however, its error accumulates nonlinearly over time due to bias and noise propagation. Fusion methods based on Extended Kalman Filters (EKF) and Particle Filters (PF) can improve average localization accuracy through probabilistic state estimation. However, these approaches typically rely on manually tuned measurement covariances. Such fixed or heuristically tuned parameters are hard to sustain across varying indoor layouts, NLOS ratios, and motion patterns, leading to limited robustness and poor generalization of measurement uncertainty modeling in heterogeneous environments. To address this limitation, this work proposes an adaptive measurement covariance scaling framework in which reliability cues are learned from historical UWB/PDR trajectories. A large teacher model is employed offline to generate temporally consistent next-position predictions from structured UWB/PDR sequences, and this behavior is distilled into a lightweight student model suitable for real-time deployment. The student model continuously regulates EKF measurement covariances based on prediction residuals, enabling environment-aware fusion without manual re-tuning. Experimental results demonstrate that the proposed KD-EKF framework significantly reduces localization error, suppresses error spikes during Line-of-Sight (LOS)/NLOS transitions, and mitigates long-term drift compared to fixed-parameter EKF, thereby improving measurement robustness across diverse indoor environments.

### [2] [Enabling 6G Wireless Communications: UWB Characterization of Corridors within the H-Band](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.18629)

TL;DR: 针对6G太比特每秒速率和毫秒级延迟需求，本研究在250-330 GHz H波段走廊场景开展了80 GHz超宽带信道测量，发现路径增益指数在-2.1至-1.6之间，证实太赫兹频段适合短距视距链路部署。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 未来第六代无线系统需提供1 Tbps数据速率和低于1 ms延迟。太赫兹频段作为毫米波以上的高频段是满足要求的 promising 策略，但更高传播损耗和经典传播机制退化使系统部署面临挑战，短距离走廊场景成为管理这些效应的关键研究方向。

Method: 在250-330 GHz H波段走廊场景进行广泛测量，首次同时研究80 GHz超宽带。通过估计建模路径增益评估大尺度效应，分析Rice K因子、均方根时延扩展和相干带宽等小尺度参数，并提出基于经典N射线模型的分析近似方法。

Result: 路径增益指数介于-2.1至-1.6，可解释为毫米波频段也存在的波导引导效应。小尺度分析显示视距主导特性，N射线模型与实证分析一致，能准确表征信道。

Conclusion: 分析表明，因视距分量相对于反射分量占主导地位，太赫兹频段适合部署点对点链路，为短距离高容量6G应用提供了理论支持。

Abstract: Future sixth-generation of wireless system is expected to provide data-rates in the order of 1 Tbps and latencies below 1 ms. Among others, one of the most promising strategies to meet these requirements is to operate at higher frequencies than millimeter wave bands: the THz bands. Nevertheless, because of the higher losses and the detriment of classical propagation mechanisms, deploying systems operating at these frequencies becomes a real challenge. Consequently, short-range scenarios are of special interest since these effects of THz bands can be managed. This work conducts an extensive campaign within corridors at frequencies within the H-band in the range from 250 GHz to 330 GHz. For the first time in literature, an ultra wideband of 80 GHz is studied simultaneously. Large scale effects are assessed by estimating and modeling path gain. The path gain exponent varies between -2.1 and -1.6, which is explained by a guiding effect also observed at millimeter wave bands. Small scale effects are also evaluated in terms of parameters such as rice $K$-factor, root mean squared delay spread and coherence bandwidth. Additionally, an analytical approximation based on the classical N-rays model is proposed obtaining an accurate representation of the wireless channel which is coherent with the empirical analysis. The full analysis reveals the suitability of these THz bands for deploying point-to-point links due to the predominance of the line-of-sight contribution respect to the reflected components.
